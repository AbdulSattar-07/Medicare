Healthcare Professional Use Only

This tool analyzes Fine Needle Aspiration (FNA) biopsy cell measurements from pathology lab reports.

The cell measurements required (radius, texture, perimeter, area, concavity, etc.) are imaging-derived features from microscopic cell analysis and are NOT patient-providable values.

This tool requires:

  • FNA biopsy results from a certified pathology laboratory
  • Cell nucleus measurements (mean, SE, and worst values)
  • Professional interpretation by qualified oncologist or pathologist

This tool is NOT for patient self-diagnosis.
